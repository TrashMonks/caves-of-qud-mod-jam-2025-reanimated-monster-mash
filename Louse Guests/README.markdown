# Louse Guests

Welcome two new varieties of bug into your heart and home!

Table lice love furniture! Drink lice love liquids in the possession of other creatures! Find these adorable buddies in the ruins and the watery areas of Qud, respectively.

Thanks to Dij for the tiles and for knowing lots of cool things about bugs!

Tested on 1.0.4.
